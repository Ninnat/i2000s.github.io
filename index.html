About This Site

My ultimate goal, following John Baez, is to make myself obsolete. I write so that I don’t have to repeat myself, and also so that I can later realize my past mistakes and correct them 1. I thought of my writings as drafts in progress, all being parts of a Long Content.

About Me

I’m a graduate student doing research in theoretical quantum information science. Everything on this site is my own viewpoint unless specified otherwise by a publication.
