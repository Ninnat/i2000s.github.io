# About This Site My ultimate goal, following John Baez, is to [make myself obsolete](http://math.ucr.edu/home/baez/TWF.html). I write so that I don't have to repeat myself, and also so that I can later realize my past mistakes and correct them [^1]. I thought of my writings as drafts in progress, all being parts of a [Long Content](http://www.gwern.net/About#long-content). # About Me I'm a graduate student doing research in theoretical quantum information science.
